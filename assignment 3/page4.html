The BMW M4 Competition is a high-performance sports coupe and convertible that features a powerful M TwinPower Turbo inline 6-cylinder engine, M xDrive all-wheel drive, and an 8-speed M Steptronic transmission. It is known for its sporty design, agile handling, and impressive acceleration, reaching 0-100 km/h in as little as 3.5 seconds. It competes with other high-end sports cars like the Audi RS5 and Chevrolet Corvette.
