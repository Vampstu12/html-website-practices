The Porsche 992 GT3 RS is a high-performance, road-legal sports car based on the 992 generation of the Porsche 911. It is designed as a track-focused weapon with an emphasis on aerodynamics and lightweight construction. Key features include a 4.0-liter flat-six engine, a huge rear wing with active aerodynamics, and a suspension derived from motorsport.
