The Audi RS 6 Avant performance is a high-performance luxury wagon featuring a potent 4.0L twin-turbo V8 engine that produces 621 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque. It offers supercar-level acceleration, achieving 0-60 mph in around 3.3 to 3.4 seconds, delivered through an 8-speed automatic transmission and quattro all-wheel drive. This model also incorporates advanced technology, lighter components to improve agility, and the practicality of an Avant wagon body style.
